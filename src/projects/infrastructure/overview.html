Key Objectives

  1. Analyse spatial distribution of travel times across the city grid.
  2. Visualise hotspots and coldspots through interactive hexagon overlays.
  3. Enable users to click on each hexagon to get detailed statistics.
  4. Provide export functionality for selected regions.

Methodology

  • Data preprocessing and cleaning using Python geopandas.
  • Polygon tessellation into equal-area hex cells.
  • Calculation of mean car travel times per cell.
  • Colour-mapping via a perceptually uniform colormap (e.g., viridis).
  • Rendering in MapLibre with click-driven pop-ups.
